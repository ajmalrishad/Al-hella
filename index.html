Welcome to Al Hella

We offer advanced Mitsubishi products, including control panels, SCADA, PLC, VFD, and remote monitoring systems, ensuring top-notch industrial performance.

About Us

Established in Dubai in 2004, Al Hella Electromechanical Works LLC undertakes electrical, industrial, and electromechanical projects, providing advanced technical solutions in the region.

Experts in Control Panel Manufacturing and Automation Solutions

We specialize in the design and manufacturing of electrical controllers for various industrial applications, ensuring compliance with the latest regulations. As an approved Mitsubishi panel manufacturer, we offer a full range of automation solutions, including SCADA, PLC, VFD, and remote monitoring systems. Our services include long-term maintenance solutions and expertise in advanced control systems.

Control Panels

Hazardous Area Solutions

We provide certified hazardous area controllers for the oil and gas industry, ensuring safety and compliance.

Automation Systems

Our expertise includes SCADA, PLC, VFD, and Soft Starter controllers, offering comprehensive automation solutions.

Remote Monitoring

We design and program controllers for remote monitoring and control, enhancing efficiency.
